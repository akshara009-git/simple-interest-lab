# Code of Conduct

We expect all contributors, reviewers, and participants to follow this code of conduct.

## Our standards
- Be respectful and professional.
- No harassment, personal attacks, or abusive language.
- Focus on constructive feedback.

## Reporting
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact the repository owner:
`your-email@example.com` (replace with your preferred contact email).

## Consequences
Violations may result in removal of comments, blocking or exclusion from the project, and reporting to platform maintainers.
